# **7/14/2026 DNeasy Blood & Tissue Kit gDNA Extraction**

## OBJECTIVES

1. *Stylaster californicus* complete lysis for gDNA extraction and isolation with the DNeasy Blood & Tissue Kit
2. Redo day one of extraction process --> this time use screw cap vial to ensure contents do not leak over night in incubator.
3. Incubate in 720 uL ATL and 80 uL Proteinase K, fragments take up more space in vial and thus require more volume I think.

## PROTOCOL

**DAY 1**

Preparation
1. Wipe all surfaces with 10% bleach, RNase away, and 70% ethanol. 
2. Warm-up incubator to 56˚C.

Lysis/Proteinase K Incubation

1. In fume hood, obtain coral fragments.
2. Fragment into large chunks with pre-sanitized mortar and pestle.
3. Transfer coral chunks to 1.5 mL SCREW CAP epi tubes. 
4. Add 720 uL of Buffer ATL and 80 ul of Proteinase K to each tube.
5. Invert to mix well. Do not vortex.
6. Incubate in a rotating incubator (Incubator Genie, speed 5) at 56˚C overnight.
