## PROTOCOL

**DAY 2**

Preparation

1. Wipe all surfaces with 10% bleach, RNase away, and 70% ethanol. 
2. Obtain samples with lysate from incubator. Transfer supernatant to new epi-tube and note volume. Lysate volume should only be up to 550 uL per tube. Split into multiple epi-tubes if volume exceeds 550 uL.
3. Aliquot and chill 100% ethanol in -20˚C. 

> 1 volume ethanol per uL lysate
> Ethanol Volume = ~ (800 uL * (# samples+2))

4. Aliquot EA buffer and place in a heating block set to 70˚C. 

> EA Buffer Volume: (60 uL*(# samples + 2)) 

RNA Removal

5. Add 2 μl RNase A (100 mg/ml) to sample, flick and spin to mix well, and incubate for 20 minutes at 37˚C in thermal cycler. Transfer supernatant to a clean 2 mL collection tube. Discard pellet. 

Bind

6. Add equal amounts Buffer AL (equal volume) and ice-cold 100% ethanol to the sample, and mix thoroughly by vortexing.

> 1 volume of each per uL lysate in tube
> - e.g. if you have 550 uL lysate in tube, add 550 uL ethanol and 550 uL Buffer AL

7. Pipette up to 650 uL of the mixture (including any precipitate) onto the DNeasy Mini spin column placed in a 2 ml collection tube (provided).

> Adding more than 650 uL risks overflowing and leaking reagent during centrifuge step, which can damage the centrifuge. 

8. Centrifuge at 6000 x g for 1 min at RT. Discard flow-through and collection tube.
9. Repeat steps 7-8 until all lysate has been spun through. Discard flow-thrugh, and tap tip of collection tube face down on kim wipes between each spin to remove as much flow-through as possible.

Wash

10. Place the DNeasy Mini spin column in a new 2 ml collection tube (provided), add 500 uL Buffer AW1, and centrifuge for 1 min at 6000 x g. Discard flow-through and collection tube.
11. Place the DNeasy Mini spin column in a new 2 ml collection tube (provided), add 500 μl Buffer AW2, and centrifuge for 3 min at 20,000 x g to dry the DNeasy membrane. Discard flow-through and place spin column in same collection tube.

> Note increased time and speed for this step!!

12. Spin once more for 1min at 20,000 x g to completely dry the column membrane.

> Ethanol may interfere with subsequent reactions. This centrifugation step ensures that no residual ethanol will be carried over during the following elution. Following the centrifugation step, remove the DNeasy Mini spin column carefully so that the column does not come into contact with the flow-through, since this will result in carryover of ethanol. 

Elute

13. Place the DNeasy Mini spin column in a clean 1.5 ml or 2 ml microcentrifuge tube and pipette 60 uL of 70˚C Buffer AE directly onto the DNeasy membrane. 
14. Incubate at room temperature for 7 min, and then centrifuge for 1 min at 6000 x g to elute.
15. Pipette eluate back onto the center of the spin column, incubate at RT for 7 minutes, then centrifuge for 1 minute at 6,000 x g to obtain a final elution volume of 60 uL. 
16. Aliquot 10 uL of sample to PCR tube for QC and place in -20˚C for future steps to prevent multiple freeze-thaw cycles.
